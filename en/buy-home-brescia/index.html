FAQ

Questions from buyers

How much does the buyer service cost?

We discuss it clearly from the first contact, with no surprises. The goal is to help you buy well: the value you save by avoiding overpaying matters more than the fee.

Do you only show your own listings?

No. The goal is to find the right home for you, not to push a property. Through the RE/MAX network, it is sometimes possible to see homes before they hit the portals.

How do you know if the price is fair?

With a micro-area analysis based on official OMI data from the Italian Revenue Agency and the property's real characteristics, not listing prices.

Can I buy before selling my current home?

Yes, and it is a common situation. It needs to be planned with the right timing and numbers: we discuss it in the initial analysis.

Do you work only in Brescia?

Brescia, Franciacorta and the lakes are the focus, but I also support buyers in Milan and Bergamo.
